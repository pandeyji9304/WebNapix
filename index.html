About Napix

Napix is an innovative app designed to improve logistics safety by detecting driver drowsiness using advanced machine learning models. It provides real-time monitoring and alerts to ensure driver safety and streamline logistics operations.

Logistics

Napix streamlines logistics operations by providing tools for real-time vehicle monitoring, automated alerts for driver drowsiness, and detailed reporting to optimize fleet management.

Drivers

Napix ensures driver safety with real-time drowsiness detection, personalized alerts, and support for safe driving practices. It helps drivers stay alert and ensures their well-being on the road.

Core Features

Drowsiness Detection

AI-powered detection of fatigue using CoreML, providing real-time alerts to drivers and logistics teams.

The system uses advanced machine learning models to analyze driver behavior and environmental factors, ensuring proactive measures are taken before accidents occur.

Driver & Vehicle Management

Effortlessly add and manage drivers, assign vehicles, and organize routes from a single interface.

This feature allows logistics managers to streamline operations, reducing delays and optimizing resource allocation for improved efficiency.

Real-Time Alerts

Receive and monitor driver drowsiness alerts instantly, ensuring prompt action.

Alerts are sent directly to the logistics dashboard and driver's app, ensuring that the response is immediate and effective.

Secure Data Management

State-of-the-art backend using Node.js and MongoDB ensures your data remains private and protected.

We employ robust encryption protocols and regular audits to ensure that sensitive information is safe from breaches.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Napix app? +

Napix is a logistics and transportation management app designed to help businesses streamline their operations, manage routes, assign vehicles, track drivers, and send real-time alerts for safety. It allows users to manage logistics-related tasks efficiently, improving the overall transportation experience.

How do I create an account on Napix? +

To create an account, download the Napix app from the App Store or Google Play, and follow the sign-up process. You'll be asked to enter your name, email, phone number, company details, and a password. After completing these details, you can start using the app.

How can I track my drivers and vehicles in real time? +

The Napix app provides real-time tracking through GPS integration. Once drivers are assigned to a route, you can track their location on the map in the app. Additionally, you‚Äôll receive real-time updates on the driver‚Äôs status, such as "Active," "Driving Safely," or "Drowsy."

How do I update my profile information? +

To update your profile, go to the 'Profile' section in the app. You can edit your personal details, contact information, and even upload or change your profile picture. These updates will be reflected in your account immediately.

What should I do if I forget my password? +

If you've forgotten your password, simply click the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen. You'll be asked to enter your registered email address, and a password reset link will be sent to your email. Follow the instructions in the email to reset your password.
